# 04 Brand System

Prepared: 2026-05-05

## Positioning

- Premium Bitcoin movement
- Grassroots credibility
- Documentary energy
- Not generic fintech

## Tone

- Clear
- human
- direct
- credible
- grounded in Kibera rather than abstract crypto language

## Visual Traits

- Dark-first shell
- near-black base with elevated surfaces
- high contrast text
- real photography over synthetic illustration where possible
- intentional use of orange, red, and green accents instead of spraying them everywhere

## Rebuild Guardrails

- Keep the design modern without looking VC-funded or anonymous.
- Avoid interchangeable crypto landing page tropes.
- Make Bitcoin feel practical, local, and lived-in.
