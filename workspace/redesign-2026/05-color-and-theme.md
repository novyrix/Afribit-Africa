# 05 Color And Theme

Prepared: 2026-05-05

## Locked Theme Direction

- Dark-first
- Near-black base
- Elevated surface layers
- High contrast text

## Core Palette

- Primary action: Bitcoin orange
- Supporting accent: Kenyan and Pan-African red
- Supporting accent: deep green
- Neutral base: charcoal and near-black tones

## Usage Rules

- Orange carries CTAs, key metrics, active states, and conversion emphasis.
- Red and green support story moments, not general chrome.
- Large surfaces stay dark and restrained.
- Legal and utility pages use the same palette with less drama.
