# 06 Typography

Prepared: 2026-05-05

## Locked Direction

- Body font: `Inter`
- Display font: `Space Grotesk`

## Rules

- Use `Inter` for body copy, labels, navigation, and legal content.
- Use `Space Grotesk` for heroes, section headings, and selective metric emphasis.
- Keep the display face consistent across all page briefs.
- Favor strong hierarchy over decorative type treatments.
