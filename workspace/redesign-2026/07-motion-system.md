# 07 Motion System

Prepared: 2026-05-05

## Locked Direction

- Restrained
- meaningful
- section-level
- accessibility-aware

## Approved Motion Types

- marquee rails
- blur and fade reveals
- spotlight and beam backgrounds used sparingly
- hover emphasis on cards and CTAs
- controlled count-up or live-stat motion where data matters

## Motion Rules

- Motion supports comprehension, pacing, and emphasis.
- No constant background noise.
- No motion that competes with documentary photography or important copy.
- Respect reduced-motion behavior on every implementation pass.
